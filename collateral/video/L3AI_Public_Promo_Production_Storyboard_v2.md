# L3AI Public Promo Production Storyboard v2

Runtime target: 3-5 minutes

Purpose: Convert the existing proof video into a production-ready launch film with cinematic pacing, product workflow visuals and clear audience-specific calls to action.

## Creative Direction

- Tone: calm, intelligent, credible, risk-aware.
- Visual rhythm: abstract problem -> product workspace -> automation workflow -> public resource proof -> audience paths -> CTA.
- Style: clean fintech interface, light/dark contrast, restrained motion, no hype charts, no fake balances.
- Music: restrained electronic pulse, no aggressive trading energy.
- Voice: measured narrator, clear pronunciation, bilingual subtitle support.

## Production Notes

- Use existing public assets for whitepaper, deck, Alpha Engine pack and video references.
- Do not show live account balances, real private wallet data, unapproved partner logos or fabricated metrics.
- Use approved UI mockups or anonymized product screenshots only.
- Keep all roadmap visuals labeled as planned or future where applicable.
- Include English master voiceover and Chinese subtitle track.
