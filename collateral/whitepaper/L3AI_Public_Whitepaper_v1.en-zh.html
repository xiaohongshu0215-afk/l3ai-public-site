Executive Summary / 执行摘要 - L3AI Public Whitepaper v1.0

Why L3AI Exists

L3AI 为什么存在

English

Digital asset users face fragmented information, technical interfaces, and uneven risk awareness. L3AI helps users read product context, wallet-aware journeys, and ecosystem information without promising financial outcomes.

L3AI keeps public communication source-backed, educational, bilingual, and review-gated. The package is designed for official resource-center distribution and does not create financial, legal, tax, or investment advice.

简体中文

数字资产用户面对信息碎片化、技术界面复杂和风险意识不均等问题。L3AI 帮助用户理解产品上下文、钱包相关路径和生态信息，但不承诺任何金融结果。

所有资料保持有来源依据、双语一致、经过审核，并避免收益承诺、无风险措辞、虚构合作方和私有数据暴露。
