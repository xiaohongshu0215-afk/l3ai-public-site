# L3AI Story Book Premium v2 Speaker Notes

Purpose: visual-first public story asset for first-time customers, media, partners and investor-style reviewers.

## Slide 01 - L3AI turns Web3 review into a guided product journey.

Open with the new positioning: L3AI is a guided review journey, not a hype-led pitch.

## Slide 02 - Users meet signals before they have a safe way to evaluate them.

Keep the pain point human and practical. The product exists because the first decision moment is too compressed.

## Slide 03 - A visitor should always know where they are in the story.

The page rhythm mirrors the user journey rather than an internal feature list.

## Slide 04 - The homepage has to prove the company before the deck does.

Explain that the site is not only a wrapper around PDFs. It is the first layer of product proof.

## Slide 05 - AI Quant explains the signal before a user forms a conclusion.

Make the safety boundary explicit: no guaranteed outcomes, no investment instruction.

## Slide 06 - Wallet moments become checkpoints instead of pressure points.

This slide protects the brand and explains the product stance around sensitive wallet contexts.

## Slide 07 - The business grows when visitors can verify the story.

Use the flywheel as business logic, not as a revenue promise.

## Slide 08 - L3AI connects public education, product proof and partner review.

Position the ecosystem as a public review architecture.

## Slide 09 - Every claim sits in a visible status lane.

This is a core maturity signal. Do not overstate future features.

## Slide 10 - The strongest public story says what it will not do.

Keep this page bold and minimal. It should feel like a brand principle, not a disclaimer dump.

## Slide 11 - Resources turn the story into downloadable evidence.

Use this as the handoff from story to evidence.

## Slide 12 - Open the story. Review the product. Verify the boundary.

Close with a practical external handoff.
