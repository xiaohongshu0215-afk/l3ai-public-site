Public FAQ

L3AI frequently asked questions.

Bilingual answers using the approved public website and press-kit language already present in the current launch package.

English

What category does L3AI belong to?

L3AI belongs to the AI-native Web3 financial intelligence category.

What problem does L3AI address?

L3AI helps users make sense of fragmented Web3 information across market context, wallet activity, product modules, ecosystem content and risk signals.

Does L3AI provide financial advice?

No. L3AI provides product information, education, AI-assisted context and risk-aware experiences. It does not provide personalized investment advice or guarantee outcomes.

What does AI do inside L3AI?

AI helps organize information, summarize context, explain product concepts and reduce information overload. Users remain responsible for reviewing information and making their own decisions.

What does wallet-aware mean?

Wallet-aware experiences connect product journeys with wallet context so users can better understand activity, access and interactions. Wallet-related experiences should always be reviewed with privacy and security awareness.

Does L3AI guarantee results?

No. Digital-asset activity involves uncertainty and risk. L3AI does not guarantee profit, performance, liquidity, access, rewards or any specific result.

What should media avoid saying?

Media language should avoid claims of guaranteed returns, investment certainty, legal endorsement, exchange endorsement or AI-driven financial outcomes.

简体中文

L3AI 属于什么品类？

L3AI 属于 AI 原生 Web3 金融智能品类。

L3AI 解决什么问题？

L3AI 帮助用户理解分散在市场语境、钱包活动、产品模块、生态内容和风险信号中的 Web3 信息。

L3AI 提供金融建议吗？

不提供。L3AI 提供产品信息、教育内容、AI 辅助语境和风险意识型体验。它不提供个性化投资建议，也不保证结果。

L3AI 中的 AI 起什么作用？

AI 用于整理信息、摘要语境、解释产品概念并减少信息过载。用户仍需自行查看信息并做出自己的判断。

钱包感知是什么意思？

钱包感知型体验将产品路径与钱包语境连接起来，帮助用户更好地理解活动、访问和交互。涉及钱包的体验应始终结合隐私和安全意识进行查看。

L3AI 会保证结果吗？

不会。数字资产活动存在不确定性和风险。L3AI 不保证利润、表现、流动性、访问、奖励或任何特定结果。

媒体表达应避免什么？

媒体表达应避免保证收益、投资确定性、法律背书、交易平台背书或 AI 驱动财务结果等说法。
