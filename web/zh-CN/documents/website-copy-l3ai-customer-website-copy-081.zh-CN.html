About

English

Page Headline

L3AI helps Web3 users understand before they act.

Mission

L3AI exists to make Web3 financial information easier to understand, easier to compare, and easier to approach with risk awareness.

Brand Story

Web3 users operate across many surfaces: wallets, protocols, products, communities, media, and market data. Each surface can be useful, but the combined experience is often noisy and difficult to evaluate. L3AI is built to help users organize that complexity through AI-assisted context, clear product education, and wallet-aware journeys.

The goal is not to remove responsibility from the user. The goal is to give users a more structured way to learn, review context, and make decisions with a clearer understanding of uncertainty.

Principles

  • Clarity over hype.
  • Context over noise.
  • Education over pressure.
  • Risk awareness over certainty.
  • Community learning over isolated speculation.

FAQ

English

What is L3AI?

L3AI is an AI-native financial intelligence platform for Web3 users. It helps users understand market context, wallet activity, product modules, ecosystem information, and risk considerations in a clearer way.

Is L3AI an investment adviser?

No. L3AI provides product information, education, AI-assisted context, and risk-aware user experiences. It does not provide personalized investment advice or guarantee any financial outcome.

What does AI do inside L3AI?

AI helps organize information, summarize context, explain product concepts, and reduce information overload. Users remain responsible for reviewing information and making their own decisions.

What does wallet-aware mean?

Wallet-aware experiences connect product journeys with wallet context so users can better understand activity, access, and interactions. Wallet-related experiences should always be reviewed with privacy and security awareness.

Does L3AI guarantee results?

No. Digital-asset activity involves uncertainty and risk. L3AI does not guarantee profit, performance, liquidity, access, rewards, or any specific result.

Who is L3AI for?

L3AI is for Web3 users who want a clearer way to understand product experiences, market context, wallet activity, ecosystem education, and community learning.

How should users approach risk?

Users should review product information carefully, understand that digital-asset activity can be volatile, and make decisions based on their own circumstances, judgment, and applicable rules.

L3AI 是什么？

L3AI 是面向 Web3 用户的 AI 原生金融智能平台。它帮助用户更清晰地理解市场语境、钱包活动、产品模块、生态信息和风险因素。

L3AI 是投资顾问吗？

不是。L3AI 提供产品信息、教育内容、AI 辅助语境和风险意识型用户体验。它不提供个性化投资建议，也不保证任何财务结果。

L3AI 中的 AI 起什么作用？

AI 用于整理信息、摘要语境、解释产品概念并减少信息过载。用户仍需自行查看信息并做出自己的判断。

钱包感知是什么意思？

钱包感知型体验将产品路径与钱包语境连接起来，帮助用户更好地理解活动、访问和交互。涉及钱包的体验应始终结合隐私和安全意识进行查看。

L3AI 会保证结果吗？

不会。数字资产活动存在不确定性和风险。L3AI 不保证利润、表现、流动性、访问、奖励或任何特定结果。

L3AI 适合谁？

L3AI 适合希望用更清晰方式理解产品体验、市场语境、钱包活动、生态教育和社区学习的 Web3 用户。

用户应如何看待风险？

用户应认真查看产品信息，理解数字资产活动可能存在波动，并基于自身情况、判断和适用规则做出决定。
